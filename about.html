About us

Studio Spire was founded in 2020 on the fundamental principle that the greatest contribution of architecture is to strengthen and vitalize our society. Our ability to discover inherent order among these complex components enables us to create projects tailored to their unique environments for our clients. Our process is not formed by any one style or formula, but rather strives to create design that is inventive yet timeless.

Each project begins with a collaboration approach, which engages the client in an integrated design process from the initial concept through the project's completion. We continue this approach in a project's execution as we endeavor to translate concepts into craft of construction. Our goal is to create a project with truly inspired design.

Company Specialization

Studio Spire specializes in Architectural design, Interior design and Construction.

Featured in the "Habitat" segment of The Business Standard (published July 18, 2023), this piece explores the powerful intersection of interior design and social media influence. As clients increasingly seek eye-catching, photo-ready spaces to drive engagement, our design philosophy has evolved to prioritize both aesthetics and experience. Potato Digital Head Office Featured in SHOWCASE Magazine (July 2023)

Selected for its innovative approach to workplace design, this feature showcases how Studio Spire met the challenge of rental space constraints to craft a dynamic, playful office for Potato Digital. From the strategic integration of the brand's signature bold red and interactive dot walls to flexible brainstorming zones and a dedicated relaxation lounge, every zone was meticulously planned. The design bridges productivity and comfort, leaving a powerful, creative impression on clients and overnight teams alike.

Studio Spire Feature in CERAMIC BANGLADESH Magazine (December 2023)

This profile features Studio Spire's rapid 45-day design and execution of the Potato Digital head office in Gulshan, a project that quickly became "the talk of the town." Breaking free from conventional cubicles, the design introduces a vibrant, open-plan layout split into distinct creative, client, and executive zones. By utilizing industrial mesh ceilings, bespoke planter boxes instead of solid partitions, and photogenic "Instagrammable" elements, the space successfully maximizes natural light and spatial transparency while driving a documented boost in team productivity.
