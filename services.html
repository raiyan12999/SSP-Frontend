What We Do

Our Services

Studio Spire specializes in Architectural design, Interior design and Construction.

Architectural Design and Consultancy

The way we design does not follow a rigid formula or cookie cutter process. We question everything during the process and are always looking for better materials, methods and faster options. We work directly with clients in a collaborative effort to meet their needs and achieve their goals.

Interior Design and Consultancy

Our entire design team collaborates with the client in defining the image and how to achieve it in the selection of colors, materials, and textures.

Architectural Drawings and Illustrations

There are number of illustration techniques we utilize depending on the proposed purpose, from conceptual sketches to highly detailed and realistic renderings, animated walkthroughs and architectural drawings. They can also assist the client in obtaining financing, or attract investors.

Construction Drawings and Documentation

Clear construction documents are critical for a variety of reason; in the bidding process to obtain clear and competitive bids; in the construction process to assure the design intent properly and concisely communicated and eventually for maintenance and repairs throughout the life of the structure .

Construction Implementation and Supervision

We have a team of builders, contractors and highly skill vendors to construct and implement each projects. During the construction period, we make ourselves available to the contractor to answer any questions and address any concern that may arise.

Construction Administration

Responsible design requires attention to sustainable and environmentally sound solutions.That is our approach to every projects. Practical solution will provide the client with added value over the years of use.
